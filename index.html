About Us

Green Wolverine

Green Wolverine is a student organization focused on the cannabis industry. Our mission is to discover opportunities for success in cannabis or related industries through education, networking, and recruiting.

  • We provide access to professional opportunities through guest speakers, networking events, and recruiting channels. Our goal is for every member to gain value through connections and inimitable knowledge. We cover a topic that is rarely mentioned in a current standard business education and is often plagued by stigmatization and misunderstanding. However, we do not take a political stance and avoid legal ambiguity by focusing only on ancillary opportunities that are completely legal on all levels of government.
